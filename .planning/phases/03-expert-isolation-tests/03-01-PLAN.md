- `logging.wandb_run_name: low-only-r16-lr5e-05`
- `sampling.enabled: true`, `sampling.every_n_epochs: 1` (sample every epoch for max visibility -- epochs 2 and 5 included, extra samples at 1,3,4 are trivial cost on 98GB GPU)
- `sampling.seed: 42`, `sampling.sample_steps: 30`, `sampling.guidance_scale: 4.0`
